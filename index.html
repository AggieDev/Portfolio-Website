Who am I? I am Waylon Brown and currently reside in Texas. I enjoy many hobbies, such as developing software, acoustic guitar, brewing beer, archery, and more. Currently I am a student at Texas A&M University pursuing my Computer Science degree (with a Business minor). Software is my passion, and Android is currently my favorite platform to develop for.

I enjoy making Android apps under my company Appuccino LLC, with the goal of making each and every app's user interfaces as clean, modern, and easy to use as possible. I emphasize quality. I never skimp on user experience, and am determined to make sure the app fits the exact vision I have for it, while not releasing it until the back-end works just as the end-user would expect.

Android development is not all I work on, however. As an example, this website has been my latest project in learning the basics of web design, and I also have had a lot of fun learning Unity3D, and currently have a mobile 3D game under production.
