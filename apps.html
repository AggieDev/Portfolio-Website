HoloConvert

HoloConvert is my newest Android app. It does unit conversions, has a tip calculator, updates currency rates to be the most recent, and much more. There are other unit conversion Android apps out there, some quite powerful, but I felt they all looked old, outdated, and were not user-friendly. I therefore made this app to feature a modern holo design, while including features in other apps and adding more as well.

HoloConvert is:
• 4.5/5 stars overall
• Was featured on
XDA-Developers

SmartSilence

SmartSilence was my very first Android app, and silences/vibrates and unsilences your phone automatically when you want it to in the week, or on a certain calendar date. At its launch, this is the only app I have made that did not exceed its expected mark as far as downloads and revenue, as it had certain uncorrected issues dealing with the Android alarm system, which would be interferred with when doing certain things such as turning off and on your phone, and at the time I was still learning many programming concepts.
